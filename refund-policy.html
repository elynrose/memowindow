Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2024

At MemoWindow, we want you to be completely satisfied with your waveform art. This Refund Policy explains our return, exchange, and refund procedures for our custom-created products.

Important: Due to the custom nature of our waveform art, all sales are generally final. However, we offer refunds and exchanges in specific circumstances as outlined below.

1. General Policy

Since each waveform art piece is custom-created from your unique audio file, all sales are typically final. However, we understand that issues can arise, and we're committed to resolving them fairly.

2. When Refunds Are Available

2.1 Production Errors

We will provide a full refund if:

  • The print quality is significantly below our standards
  • The waveform was generated incorrectly from your audio file
  • The wrong size or format was produced
  • The product arrives damaged due to our error

2.2 Shipping Issues

We will provide a full refund if:

  • The package is lost in transit (after investigation period)
  • The product arrives damaged due to shipping
  • We ship to the wrong address due to our error

2.3 Service Issues

We will provide a full refund if:

  • We are unable to process your audio file due to technical issues
  • Processing takes significantly longer than promised
  • We cannot fulfill your order for any reason

3. When Refunds Are Not Available

Refunds are not available for:

  • Change of mind after order placement
  • Dissatisfaction with the waveform appearance (as it's based on your audio)
  • Audio quality issues in the original file
  • Incorrect shipping address provided by customer
  • Orders cancelled after production has begun
  • Digital downloads or processed files

4. Refund Process

How to Request a Refund:

  1. Contact Us: Email support@memorywindow.com within 30 days of delivery
  2. Provide Details: Include your order number and reason for refund request
  3. Submit Evidence: If applicable, provide photos of damaged items or quality issues
  4. Review Process: We will review your request within 2-3 business days
  5. Resolution: We will provide a response and next steps

5. Refund Timeline

5.1 Processing Time

Once approved, refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days. The time for the refund to appear in your account depends on your bank or credit card provider.

5.2 Refund Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

6. Exchanges

6.1 Size Exchanges

We may offer size exchanges in limited circumstances:

  • If the wrong size was produced due to our error
  • If the product is defective
  • Customer pays the price difference for larger sizes

6.2 Exchange Process

For approved exchanges:

  • Return the original item in original condition
  • We will produce the new item
  • Standard shipping times apply to the replacement

7. Return Shipping

7.1 Our Error

If the return is due to our error, we will provide a prepaid return shipping label.

7.2 Customer Error

If the return is due to customer error, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs.

8. Digital Products

Digital products, including processed waveform files and digital downloads, are non-refundable once delivered.

9. Cancellation Policy

9.1 Before Production

Orders can be cancelled with a full refund if cancelled before production begins (typically within 24 hours of order placement).

9.2 After Production

Orders cannot be cancelled once production has begun, as the custom product has already been created.

10. Quality Guarantee

We guarantee:

  • High-quality printing on premium materials
  • Accurate waveform generation from your audio
  • Careful packaging and shipping
  • Responsive customer service

11. Dispute Resolution

If you are not satisfied with our resolution of your refund request, you may contact us to escalate the matter. We are committed to fair resolution of all disputes.

12. International Orders

For international orders, refund policies may vary based on local laws and regulations. Please contact us for specific information about your region.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
